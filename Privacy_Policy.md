## 2. Information We Collect and Receive

We collect Personal Data from you in various ways when you use the Services.

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you use the Services, including:

### 2.2 Information We Automatically Collect

We may automatically collect certain technical and usage information when you use the Services.

#### 2.2.1 Log and Usage Information

This may include:
	
* Internet Protocol (IP) address
* Date and time of access
* *Application version
* Operating system and version
* Referring and exit pages
* *Feature usage and interaction events

#### 2.2.2 Error and Diagnostic Information (via Sentry)

When the Services experience an error, crash, or performance issue, we may collect diagnostic data through Sentry, including:
	
* Error logs and stack traces
* Device and operating system information
* Application configuration
* Time and date of the error

This information is used solely to:
	
* Diagnose and resolve technical problems
* Improve system stability and performance

We do not intentionally collect your input text or AI-generated content through Sentry.

#### 2.2.3 Usage Analytics (via Google Analytics 4)

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to collect anonymized or aggregated usage data, such as:
	
* Session duration
* Feature interaction frequency
* Navigation behavior
* Button clicks and interface interactions

This information is used to:

* Understand how users use the Services
* Improve usability and functionality
* Optimize product design

We do not collect the actual text content you input or generate using GA4.

You may use the Services to process text and submit such text to third-party AI providers (such as OpenAI, Google, Anthropic, or others).
We do not store this content on our servers. Processing of such information is governed by the privacy policies of those third-party providers.
